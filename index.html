Album
✨GOLDEN - JungKook 2023

Jungkook's song "Seven" is a smooth, R&B-inspired track released on July 14, 2023. Featuring Latto, the song explores themes of love, passion, and devotion, with Jungkook expressing his commitment to being with his partner "seven days a week." Its catchy melody, sultry vibe, and heartfelt lyrics resonate with fans, blending Jungkook's vocals with Latto's dynamic rap. The song gained massive popularity, topping charts globally, showcasing Jungkook's versatility as a solo artist outside of BTS.
